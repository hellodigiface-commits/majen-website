Impact & Inclusion

Majen builds digital and operational infrastructure that connects Africa's fashion creators to global commerce — in the most equitable, culturally grounded, and sustainable way. Aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals, we advance decent work, gender equity, and inclusive innovation to ensure African fashion businesses built for the long term.

How We Create Impact

We translate global development goals into practical tools, systems, and opportunities that directly support fashion creators and creative economies across Africa.

Aligned with UN SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth
Majen enables independent fashion businesses to access global markets, manage orders, receive secure payments, and coordinate international shipping. By formalizing and digitizing fashion services, we support job creation, income stability, and long-term business growth for creators across the continent.

Aligned with UN SDG 10: Reduced Inequalities
Majen was built to lower barriers for African creators entering the global fashion ecosystem. Through streamlined onboarding, locally relevant tools, and support for both custom and ready-to-wear orders, we make cross-border fashion commerce more accessible and equitable.

Aligned with UN SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure
We provide creators with essential digital infrastructure, including customer management tools, order tracking, communication features, and data insights that are typically unavailable to small fashion businesses. This access strengthens digital literacy, business resilience, and innovation within local creative economies.

Aligned with UN SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production
By prioritizing made-to-order and custom fashion, Majen promotes intentional production over mass manufacturing. We help preserve traditional craftsmanship while encouraging sustainable practices that value quality, longevity, and cultural authenticity.

Aligned with UN SDG 5: Gender Equality
Women make up a significant share of Africa's fashion workforce. By increasing visibility, access to global customers, and income-generating opportunities for women-led businesses, Majen supports greater economic agency and financial independence across the fashion value chain.
