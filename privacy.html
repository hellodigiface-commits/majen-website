1. Introduction

Welcome to Majen ("the App"), a product of Majen Technologies ("Majen," "we," "us," or "our"). Majen is a fashion technology platform that connects African fashion service providers ("Designers") with shoppers ("Clients") seeking custom and ready-to-wear garments. We are currently operating in Nigeria and the United States.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the Majen mobile application and any related services (collectively, the "Services"). It applies to all users of the App, including both Designers and Clients.

By creating an account or using the Majen App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Minimum Age: The Majen App is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have collected information from a person under 18, we will promptly delete that information and terminate their account.

2. Who This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy applies to two types of users:

Clients: Individuals who download and use the Majen App to browse, purchase, or place custom and ready to wear orders for garments from Designers.

Designers: Fashion service providers which includes those offering custom garment creation and ready-to-wear items who register on the Majen App to list and sell their products and services.

Both Clients and Designers are referred to collectively as "users," "you," or "your" in this policy, except where we distinguish between the two.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide to Us

When you register an account (Clients and Designers):

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Password (stored in encrypted form)
  • Profile photo (optional)

When you sign in via a third-party service:

If you choose to register or log in using Google, Apple, or Facebook, we receive certain information from those platforms, such as your name, email address, and profile picture, in accordance with your settings on those platforms. We do not receive your passwords for those services.

When Designers set up their profile:

  • Business or brand name
  • Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) Registration Number (RC Number): We collect this to verify that Designers are registered businesses in Nigeria before they are permitted to onboard onto the Majen platform. This information is used for verification purposes only and is stored securely.
  • Portfolio photos and images of garments
  • Service descriptions, pricing, and availability
  • Business social media links
  • Bank account or payout details (processed securely through our payment partner, Flutterwave)

When Clients set up their profile and use the App:

  • Shipping addresses, including any saved or default delivery addresses stored on your account
  • Body measurements (e.g., bust, waist, hips, height, and other sizing information) that you voluntarily provide to facilitate custom garment orders. This is sensitive personal data and is stored securely and used only to fulfill your orders.
  • Wishlist items: garments and Designer listings you save for future reference
  • Custom order specifications (e.g., style preferences, fabric choices, reference images)
  • Order history and transaction records

Regarding stored payment details: If you choose to save your payment details for future purchases, this information is stored securely by our payment processor, Flutterwave, and not on Majen's servers. We store only a tokenized reference that allows Flutterwave to process future payments on your behalf. You may remove stored payment details at any time through your account settings.

When you use the in-app chat feature:

  • Messages exchanged between Clients and Designers through the Majen messaging system
  • The date, time, and participants of each conversation
  • Any photos or files shared within the chat

Please note that in-app messages are used solely to facilitate communication between Clients and Designers regarding orders and services. We may review chat content where necessary to investigate disputes, enforce our Terms of Service, or comply with legal obligations. You should not share sensitive personal or financial information (such as card numbers or passwords) through the chat feature.

When you contact us:

Any information you include in messages, feedback, or support requests sent to us.

3.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Usage data: Pages and features accessed, time spent in the App, clicks, items viewed, search queries, garments saved or favorited, cart activity, and other interaction data that helps us understand how users engage with the App and its content
  • Consumer behavior analytics: We collect data on shopping patterns, browsing habits, order preferences, and interactions with Designer listings to generate insights into consumer behavior. This data is used to improve our platform, personalize your experience, and help Designers better understand demand for their products. Where shared with Designers, this data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.
  • Log data: IP address, app crash reports, and diagnostic information
  • Location data: With your permission, we may collect approximate or precise location data to help connect you with Designers in your area or to facilitate delivery. You may disable location access at any time through your device settings.

3.3 Payment Information

We do not collect or store your raw payment card details on our servers. All payment processing is handled by Flutterwave, our third-party payment processor. When you make a payment, your card information is submitted directly to Flutterwave and is governed by Flutterwave's Privacy Policy. If you choose to save your payment details for future use, Flutterwave stores your card data and provides Majen with a secure token only, which means we never have access to your raw card numbers. We receive only a transaction confirmation and limited non-sensitive details such as the last four digits of your card and the card type. You may remove stored payment details at any time through your account settings.

3.4 Photos and Images

  • Clients may upload photos to communicate style preferences or reference images for custom orders.
  • Designers upload photos of their garments, fabrics, sketches, portfolio work, and profile images.
  • All images uploaded to Majen are stored securely and used only for the purposes described in this policy.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Facilitate transactions and communication between Clients and Designers, including through the in-app chat feature
  • Enable Designers to showcase their work and receive orders
  • Verify Designer eligibility for onboarding using CAC RC Numbers
  • Enable Clients to browse, shop, place custom/ready to wear orders, and manage their wishlists and saved addresses
  • Use body measurements provided by Clients solely to facilitate accurate custom garment orders
  • Process payments and send order confirmations and receipts
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send important account and service-related notifications (e.g., order updates, account alerts)
  • Send promotional communications, where you have opted in to receive them
  • Analyze consumer behavior and shopping trends to improve our platform, personalize recommendations, and provide Designers with aggregated, anonymized insights about demand for their products and services
  • Improve and personalize the App experience
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends to enhance our Services
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activity
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations in Nigeria and the United States
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

5.1 Between Users on the Platform

When a Client places an order with a Designer, we share the information necessary to fulfill that order, such as the Client's name and order specifications with the relevant Designer. Designers' public profile information, portfolio photos, and service listings are visible to all Clients on the App.

5.2 With Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party companies that help us operate the App, including:

  • Flutterwave: Payment processing
  • Cloud hosting providers: Secure data storage and infrastructure
  • Analytics providers: App performance and usage analysis
  • Authentication providers: Google, Apple, and Facebook for social login

These service providers are authorized to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us and are required to protect your information in accordance with applicable law.

5.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if we believe in good faith that doing so is necessary to:

  • Comply with applicable law, regulation, or legal process
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of Majen, our users, or the public
  • Respond to a valid request from law enforcement or government authorities in Nigeria or the United States

5.4 Business Transfers

If Majen Technologies is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent notice in the App before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5.5 With Your Consent

We may share your information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our Services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal, regulatory, or fraud prevention purposes.

Order records and transaction data may be retained for up to 10 years in accordance with applicable financial recordkeeping requirements.
