Meet the Founders

Ifeoma Azinge

Ifeoma Azinge is the former founder of Joluwe, a fashion rental startup producing African-made clothing for U.S. customers, and is completing her PhD in Neuroscience at Brown University. She has deep expertise in African fashion and manufacturing, and a strong technical background in building creative, data-driven solutions for complex problems.

Nadia Udochi

Nadia Udochi holds an MSc in Development Management from London School of Economics and a Master in International Energy Transitions from Sciences Po, Paris. She specializes in African development, particularly the creative and extractive sectors, and has a proven track record of building strategic partnerships that drive sustainable growth.
