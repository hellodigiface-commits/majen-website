What is Majen?

Majen is a fashion technology company that is transforming how African fashion providers reach global clients and carry out international orders.

Our first product, the Majen app, takes the stress out of shopping and collaborating across borders. Customers can safely connect with vetted designers and manufacturers, place secure orders, and shop curated African brands, all while enjoying simplified logistics, payments and subsidized shipping.

A two side digital platform enabling discovery, custom orders, secure payments, simplified logistics, and messaging
